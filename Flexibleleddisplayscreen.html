Customized Shape LED Screen

Customized shape LED screens are flexible, modular displays designed to fit unique forms such as circles, spheres, logos, and more. These screens utilize soft PCBs and magnetic mounting systems, enabling seamless integration into creative architectural and branding projects.

Circular LED Screen

Circular LED screens, also known as round or creative LED displays, offer a unique and visually striking solution for various applications, including architectural installations, retail displays, and event setups.

Kinetic Systems LED Screen

Kinetic LED screens are dynamic display systems that combine traditional LED technology with mechanical movement, creating immersive, three-dimensional visual experiences. These systems are widely used in public spaces, retail environments, and entertainment venues to captivate audiences with their innovative design and functionality.

Taxi Top Display

Taxi Top LED Displays are dynamic advertising platforms mounted on the roofs of vehicles, offering high visibility and mobility for advertisers. These displays are particularly effective in urban environments, allowing brands to reach a wide audience through moving billboards.
